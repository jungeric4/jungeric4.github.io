Overview

The objective was to build a sensor capable of measuring thickness of a coin. The sensor that was used for this project was a rotary encoder. An Arduino was used as an interface, and an LCD display was used to output the converted displacement. To convert rotational motion into slow rectilinear motion of the clamp head, a worm gear with large radius was used, providing significant mechanical advantage. The sensor provided an accuracy of 0.1mm.
